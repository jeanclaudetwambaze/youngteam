Child Sport Program

This system is designed to provide detailed information about children and their activities, categorized into various tabs such as BIO, Activities, Physical Exercise, Age Categories, Holiday Program, and School Program.

BIO

The BIO section contains personal details about each child, including the names of their parents, guardians, age, and location. View BIO

Activities

The Activities tab includes a list of activities such as Basketball, with details on drills, shooting, dribbling, and passing. View Activities

Physical Exercise

This section provides details on various physical exercises like Foot Fire, Plank, Pushups, Running, and Stretching. View Physical Exercises

Age Categories

The Age Categories tab categorizes children into different age groups, including Under 13 Boys & Girls, Under 15 Boys & Girls, and Under 18 Boys & Girls. View Age Categories

Holiday Program (School Break)

In the Holiday Program section, you can find the schedule for activities conducted during school breaks, typically from 8:00 AM to 9:30 AM, Monday to Thursday. View Holiday Program

School Program (No Break)

The School Program tab outlines the schedule for activities during school days, with specific time slots for different age groups. View School Program
